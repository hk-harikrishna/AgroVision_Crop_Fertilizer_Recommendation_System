About AgroVision

AgroVision is a machine learning-based system that recommends the best crop to grow and fertilizers to use based on environmental factors, with the aim of helping farmers optimize their crop yields and reduce waste. By analyzing soil data, weather patterns, and other environmental factors, the system generates personalized crop and fertilizer recommendations for farmers, providing insights on optimal crop varieties and resource management.
